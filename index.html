About Us

Founded in 1973, Shreenathji Seeds Farm has been a trusted name in providing high-quality vegetable seeds for over 51 years. From a small family business, we have become a leader in the agricultural industry, dedicated to helping farmers achieve abundant, nutritious harvests through superior seeds and sustainable practices.

Our seeds are rigorously tested for quality and adaptability to Indian climates, ensuring optimal yields. We proudly offer a diverse range of eco-friendly, vegetarian seeds, supporting farmers of all scales. Rooted in tradition and innovation, we continue to uphold the values of quality, trust, and sustainability for a greener future.

Our Range of Products

Celebrating over 51 years of excellence in the agricultural industry, Shreenathji Seeds Farm has been a trusted name in cultivating and distributing high-quality seeds across India. Since our inception in 1973, we have been committed to empowering farmers with the best seeds for optimum yield and sustainable farming practices.

At Shreenathji Seeds Farm, we specialize in providing a wide variety of seeds, including 45 premium vegetable seeds and 15 top-quality field crop seeds. Our seeds are meticulously selected to ensure the highest purity, germination, and productivity. We pride ourselves on the success of our farmers and continue to innovate and provide solutions for sustainable agriculture.

Research And Development

At Shreenathji Seeds Farm, we believe that continuous innovation is the cornerstone of agricultural progress. Over the years, our Research & Development (R&D) division has been at the forefront of discovering and implementing new technologies to enhance seed quality, boost yields, and promote sustainable farming practices.

Our R&D efforts are focused on developing seeds that are not only high-yielding but also resilient to the challenges posed by changing climate conditions, diseases, and pests. We take pride in combining traditional wisdom with modern science to offer seeds that meet the evolving needs of farmers across India.
